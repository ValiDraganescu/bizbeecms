# CMS v2.2.1

_2026-07-23 · changes since r-2.2.0_

## Fixes
- **Translating one block field no longer clears its neighbours.** In the Page Builder block inspector, running AI-translate on a field (e.g. a SectionHeading's title) was wiping the block's other translatable fields (e.g. the subtitle) — and the loss was saved, not just visual. Translation now preserves every other prop on the block.
